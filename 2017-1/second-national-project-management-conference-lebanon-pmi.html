May the Passion and Ambition we have felt during the celebration of the Project Management Day in Lebanon be and abide with us as we go about those things which occupy us each day.

What a wonderful conference this has been. No matter how experienced we were, there was always room for everyone at The Second National Project Management Conference 2015 organized by PMI Lebanon Chapter on November 12. Our knowledge became more up-to-date by attending a wide range of technical workshops in project management, we have been fed by positive energy and leadership motivation as we have listened to the inspired words and success stories of the men and women who have addressed us.

Mrs. Claude Khalil, PMI Lebanon Chapter

After the team hard work during the preparation for this conference, I knew this was going to be something special from the opening session till the closing classy ceremony during the lunch; from the opening word to the technical workshops to the success stories of cutting edge organizations and governmental projects. it was a superb opportunity for us to listen to inspiring talks from prominent project management leaders and to know all there is, about project management, the impact it is having today, its current realities, and how it is going to take shape in the future.

The technical workshops covered hot topics that touches all project managers and leaders including the ‘PMI Talent Triangle’ the new PDU approach, the Cloud Project Management, the CMMI and ISO implementation and many others.

Abul Kamal PMI Region Mentor and PMI Lebanon Chapter Gretta Kelzi, Pierre Hervouet, Hasan Jaffal and Walid Fenianos

One funny thing to remember is the selfiesbox which was a booth during the conference and the restaurant where attendees captured selfie images and published them to Facebook, check out the album full of fun.

Until we see you in The Third National Project Management Conference 2017, we will be waiting your suggestions, comments and what and who you would like to see in the next year.
